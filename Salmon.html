Air Fry Salmon

Ingredients

  • 1 pound salmon cut into bite-size pieces
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon freshly grated ginger
  • 2 tablespoon Soy Sauce
  • 1.5 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 3 tablespoon Japanese mayo
  • Salt
  • Pepper

  • Honey Glaze
  • 1.5 tablespoon honey
  • 2.5 tablespoon Soy Sauce
  • 0.5 tablespoon freshly grated ginger
  • ¼ cup water

    • Corn Starch Slurry

  • 1 tbsp corn starch
  • 2 tbsp cold water
@dinewiththefine_ @dinewiththefine_ @dinewiththefine_

Instructions

Marinate the salmon with honey, freshly grated ginger, soy sauce, oyster sauce, Japanese mayo, salt, and pepper for 20 minutes.

Air fry at 380F for 12 minutes, flipping halfway through.

Optional: Make a honey glaze sauce to drizzle over the salmon.

Add soy sauce, honey, ginger, and water to a pot. Bring it to a simmer on medium-high heat for 2-3 minutes or until bubbly.

Add corn starch and water slurry to thicken, and simmer for another 1 minute.

Drizzle the sauce over the salmon, and garnish with sesame seeds.
