Gooey Chocolate Raspberry Brownies

Ingredients

      
  • 250g salted butter, cut into chunks
    •   
  • 200g 70% dark chocolate, broken into pieces
    •   
  • 4 medium eggs
    •   
  • 350g caster sugar
    •   
  • 200g plain flour
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • Sea salt
    •   
  • 170g frozen raspberries
  •   
@dinewiththefine_ @dinewiththefine_ @dinewiththefine_

Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 160c fan. Chuck the butter, chocolate and a small pinch of salt into a saucepan and melt together

slowly for a few minutes over a low heat, giving it a stir every now and then to help it come together.

2. Meanwhile, beat the sugar together with the eggs with an electric whisk for about 5 minutes until the mixture feel pretty

smooth. You're trying to dissolve as much of ethe sugar as possible to get a really swirly top to the finished brownies.

3. Pour the melted chocolate and butter into the sugar and egg mixture and mix together. Add the flour and coco powder and mix

together. Pour into a lined 8x10 inch baking tin and bake for 28-30 minutes or until the sides of the brownie are pulling

away and a skewer comes out a little dirty from the sides.

4. Cool in the fridge for 4 hours or overnight. Slice them chilled so you get neat squares and then leave for 2-3 hours to come to

room temperature before serving.
