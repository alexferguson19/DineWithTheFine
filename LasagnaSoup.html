Lasagna Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 yellow onion, chopped
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 24 ounce jar tomato sauce
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flake
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 2-3 basil leaves
  • 10 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese, grated
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • Lasagna noodles, broken into small pieces
  • 2 cups fresh spinach leaves

Instructions

In a large pot on medium high heat, brown ground beef with salt & pepper.

Add onion to the same pan & cook until softened. Add garlic, mix until fragrant.

Add tomato paste, cook down & season with Italian seasoning, red pepper flakes & oregano.

Pour your favorite tomato sauce along with broth & basil leaves.

Let it come to a boil before adding in lasagna noodles. Once pasta is cooked, add spinach and mozzarella.

Serve with a dollop of ricotta cheese mixture and some fresh basil, parsley & Parmesan.
