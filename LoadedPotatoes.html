Loaded Sheredded Chicken Spud Bowl

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Chicken Breast

    • Sauce

  • Mayonnaise
  • BBQ Sauce
  • Spring Onion
  • Grated Cheddar
@dinewiththefine_ @dinewiththefine_ @dinewiththefine_

Instructions

Potatoes:

Soak dice potatoes in water then let it sit.

Put potatoes in air fryer and season it with Fried Potato Mix from Flying Tiger shop, All Purpose Seasoning, Italian Herb Seasoning, Paprika and a bit of oil.

Put in air fryer for 25 minutes at 190°.

Chicken:

Fry on a frying pan the chicken for 3 minutes both sides on 7 heat, then cook with lid on for 10 minutes on 4 heat.

Then shred the chicken using 2 forks.
