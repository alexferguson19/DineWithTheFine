About Me

Hi there! My name is Alexandria Ferguson, and I’m so excited to welcome you to my little corner of the internet.

I’m currently a student, balancing my studies with one of my biggest passions: cooking.

Ever since I was young, I’ve loved experimenting in the kitchen — from classic comfort foods to creative new dishes inspired by flavors around the world.

Cooking isn't just a hobby for me; it’s a way to bring people together, tell stories, and create unforgettable memories.

Through Dine With The Fine, I hope to share recipes that are not only delicious but also easy and fun for everyone to make.

Whether you’re a beginner or a seasoned home chef, I want this space to inspire you to get creative, enjoy the process, and savor every bite.

Thank you for being part of this journey with me — let’s cook, learn, and celebrate good food together!
