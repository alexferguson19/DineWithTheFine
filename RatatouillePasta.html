Ratatouille Chickken Pasta

Ingredients

    Ratatouille

  • 1 Aubergine
  • 1 Courgette
  • 1 Tomato
  • 1 Onion
  • 3 Cloves of Garlic
  • 1 Tin of Tomatoes
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Chili
  • Paprika/li>
  • Herbes De Provence

    • Side

  • Chicken
  • Pasta
@dinewiththefine_ @dinewiththefine_ @dinewiththefine_

Instructions

1. Prepare Ingredients:

Dice the aubergine (eggplant), courgette (zucchini), and tomato into bite-sized pieces.

Finely chop the onion and mince the 3 cloves of garlic.

Season the chicken with a pinch of salt, pepper, paprika, and Herbes de Provence.

2. Cook the Chicken:

In a large pan, heat a little oil over medium heat.

Cook the chicken pieces until golden brown and cooked through (about 6–8 minutes).

Remove the chicken from the pan and set aside.

3. Sauté the Vegetables:

In the same pan, add a little more oil if needed.

Add the chopped onion and garlic, cooking until softened and fragrant (about 2–3 minutes).

Add the diced aubergine, courgette, and tomato. Stir and cook for another 5–7 minutes until they start to soften.

4. Add Tomatoes and Seasonings:

Pour in the tin of tomatoes.

Season generously with salt, pepper, chili, paprika, and Herbes de Provence to taste.

Let everything simmer together for 10–15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens and the vegetables are tender.

5. Cook the Pasta:

While the sauce simmers, cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente.

Drain the pasta, reserving a small cup of pasta water.

6. Combine Everything:

Add the cooked chicken back into the ratatouille sauce.

Toss the pasta into the sauce, adding a splash of the reserved pasta water if needed to loosen it up.

Stir everything well so the pasta gets coated in all that saucy goodness!

7. Serve and Enjoy:

Serve hot, optionally topping with a little fresh parsley, Parmesan cheese, or extra herbs if you like!
