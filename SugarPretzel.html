Cinnamon Sugar Pretzel

Ingredients

    Pretzel

      
  • 3 teaspoons instant dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 2 tablespoons granulated white sugar
  • 4 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda

    • Cinnamon Sugar Coating

      
  • 1/2 cup granulated white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon pumpkin pie seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/2 Cup (1 stick) melted butter

Instructions

In a large bowl, combine the yeast, warm water and sugar. Add in the flour and salt and knead until smooth using hands.

Grease a second bowl with nonstick cooking spray and place the dough into the bowl. Cover with a clean dish towel and let rise for 30 minutes in a warm spot.

Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F. Line 2-baking sheets with parchment paper.

Divide the dough into 8 equal parts. Roll each part into a rope about 24 to 26 inches long and form into a pretzel shape

In a shallow bowl or plate, combine water and baking soda. Submerge each pretzel in the water and then place on the prepared baking sheets. Sprinkle with salt. Let the pretzels rest for 5 to 10 minutes.

Bake 12 to 15 minutes, or until golden brown.

Move the pretzels to a cooling rack.

Dip the pretzels into the melted butter and then into the sugar mixture. These pretzels are best served the same day.
