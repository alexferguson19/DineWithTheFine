Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 2 Pancetta
  • 1 Spaghetti
  • 1 Onion
  • 3 Garlic
  • 3 Eggs
  • Bit of Cream
  • 60g Parmigiano
  • Pepper

Instructions

1. Prepare Ingredients:

Dice the pancetta.

Finely chop the onion and garlic.

Grate the Parmigiano.

Crack the 3 eggs into a bowl, add a bit of cream, and whisk together until smooth. Stir in the grated Parmigiano and lots of black pepper. Set aside.

2. Cook the Pasta:

Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.

Add the spaghetti and cook until al dente (according to package instructions).

Before draining, save 1 cup of pasta water.

3. Cook the Pancetta and Veggies:

In a large pan over medium heat, cook the diced pancetta until crispy.

Add the chopped onion and garlic, sautéing until soft and fragrant (about 2–3 minutes).

4. Combine Everything:

Turn the heat off under the pan (important to prevent scrambling the eggs).

Add the hot drained spaghetti into the pan with pancetta, onions, and garlic. Toss well.

Quickly pour the egg, cream, and Parmigiano mixture over the pasta.

Stir vigorously, adding a splash of the reserved pasta water to make a creamy sauce if needed.

Season with extra black pepper to taste.

5. Serve:

Plate immediately, with extra Parmigiano on top if you like!
